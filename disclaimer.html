Fiction, parody, and institutional unreliability

The Office of Alternate Academia is a fictional and satirical creative project. The institution, its officials, its decrees, its crises, and most of its administrative structures do not exist outside this website and its related media content.

1. Fictional setting

All articles, reports, character profiles, institutional announcements, and narrative elements published on this site are part of a parody universe. They are intended as satire, creative writing, and humorous commentary on academic culture, bureaucracy, and research life.

2. No official institutional status

This website does not represent a real university, research office, accreditation authority, or administrative body. Any resemblance to existing institutions, persons, policies, or events is coincidental, exaggerated for comic effect, or part of the satirical intent of the project.

3. Characters and authors

Characters appearing on this site — including but not limited to Supreme Grand Rector Infinitum, Reviewer 2, Dean Bugsy, Astrid Vox, Clara Quark, and others — are fictional characters or stylised satirical personas created for narrative and entertainment purposes.

4. External platforms and media

The Office of Alternate Academia may also publish fictional content through external platforms such as YouTube, Instagram, or other social media channels. The same fictional and satirical context applies there unless explicitly stated otherwise.

5. Use of external news sources

Some articles published on this website may be inspired by real-world news reporting from external media outlets. In such cases, the original source will be credited and linked where appropriate.

These articles should be understood as satirical reinterpretations, commentary, or fictional narrative extensions within the universe of the Office of Alternate Academia. They do not attempt to reproduce or replace the original reporting.

6. Interpretation

Nothing on this site should be interpreted as legal, academic, institutional, or administrative advice. Readers are encouraged to treat all ceremonial decrees with caution, especially when issued by authorities with glowing goggles or retroactive tenure.

7. Final note

If a statement on this website appears disturbingly plausible, that is a reflection on reality, not a confirmation that the institution is real.
