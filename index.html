About the portal

A satirical publication from the Office of Alternate Academia

The Office of Alternate Academia is a fictional institution dedicated to exploring the strange, bureaucratic, and occasionally cosmic side of academic life. This website presents satirical news reports, fictional announcements, and character-driven stories from a universe where administrative logic has evolved into its own independent reality.

All articles, characters, and events published here are part of a creative parody project. While some themes may resemble real academic culture, the institution itself — and most of its policies — should not be interpreted as real.

This website is designed to be simple, transparent, and respectful of visitors' privacy. It does not collect personal data, use advertising trackers, or run profiling systems. In that sense, it follows the core principles of privacy-conscious and GDPR-aligned web publishing.

If you encounter a decree from the Supreme Grand Rector Infinitum, you may safely assume that it is fictional. If it somehow resembles reality, that is purely coincidental.
